The Person Behind It

Who I Am

I don't build companies to fit into markets. I build them to redefine what the market thought was possible.

My Story

Before Martaucy, I spent 23 years in the U.S. Army — in logistics, operations, and training development. That's where I learned how to build things that hold up under pressure. Today I work as a campus AI strategist and instructional designer while finishing my PhD in Learning Technologies, and Martaucy LLC is where all of it comes together: a holding company for ventures that challenge conventions, each one precision-built for the industry it lives in.

How I Think

I believe diversity is strength, and that the best ideas are the ones everyone can actually use. Whether I'm making emerging technology approachable for people who never thought it was "for them," or building a brand from the ground up, the goal is the same — keep it practical, keep it human, and never lose the standard. Each venture stands on its own, but they all share one DNA and one relentless drive forward.

My Vision

Creativity meets strategy. That's the model. Every decision is intentional, every venture is architected to lead rather than follow, and innovation isn't a department — it's the foundation everything is built on.

Why I Build

I'm not interested in ventures that simply exist. I build companies designed to elevate their markets — to set new standards from day one and leave a lasting mark long after the launch.

What We're Here To Do

My Mission

To create, inspire, and elevate the brands and visionaries I work with — building precision-engineered ventures that turn ideas into impact, and impact into lasting cultural momentum. Success, for me, was never just about business. It's about building things that challenge the status quo and outlast the trends that chase it.
